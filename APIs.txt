Specific Information about the APIs:

    CoinBase:
        CoinBase gives you three different values for each of its cryptocurrencies:
            Sell - The price at which it's selling the cryptocurrency
            Spot - The "spot" price of the cryptocurrency
            Buy  - The price at which it's buying back the cryptocurrency
        CoinBase currently (as of Jul 30, 2018) supports the following cryptocurrencies (https://support.coinbase.com/customer/en/portal/articles/2630943-supported-digital-currencies):
            BTC: Bitcoin
            LTC: Litecoin
            ETH: Ethereum
            BCH: Bitcoin Cash

The actual APIs themselves:

    CoinBase:
        Examples:
            Base URL:       https://api.coinbase.com/v2/prices/
            Example Buy:    https://api.coinbase.com/v2/prices/BTC-USD/buy
            Example Sell:   https://api.coinbase.com/v2/prices/BTC-USD/sell
            Example Spot:   https://api.coinbase.com/v2/prices/BTC-USD/spot
        Example Response (from 'buy'):
            {
                "data": {
                    "base": "BTC",
                    "currency": "USD",
                    "amount": "7970.12"
                }
            }

    CoinMarketCap:
        Accepted cryptocurrencies (to convert TO):
            BTC, ETH, XRP, LTC, BCH
        Accepted fiat currencies (to convert TO):
            AUD, BRL, CAD, CHF, CLP, CNY, CZK, DKK, EUR, GBP, HKD, HUF, IDR, ILS, INR, JPY, KRW, MXN, MYR, NOK, NZD,
            PHP, PKR, PLN, RUB, SEK, SGD, THB, TRY, TWD, ZAR
        Cryptocurrencies and their respective ids:
            BTC:    1
            ETH:    1027
            LTC:    2
            XRP:    57
        Examples:
            Base URL:           https://api.coinmarketcap.com/v2/ticker/
            Example BTC/USD:    https://api.coinmarketcap.com/v2/ticker/1/?convert=USD
            Example ETH/EUR:    https://api.coinmarketcap.com/v2/ticker/1027/?convert=EUR
